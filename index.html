Initiative for Modular and Upgradable Mini and All-In-One PC

This is an Open Source, Non-Profit Organization on GitHub that creates, compiles and catalogs projects, know-how articles, standards, blue prints and applications for the purpose to make Mini and All-In-One Computer modular and ugradable. For the benefit of the individual DIY person as well as the industry as a whole. We have published what we have under CC and MIT License and we are working on closing all holes. Since this is Open Source, you are invited to help. Or simply use what is already available. Pay attention to the progress bars and ribbons.
