Modular PC Open Source

Upgradable Mini and All-In-One PC

V-Squared is an Open Source, Non-Profit Standard Initiative and Community on GitHub.com/V-Squared that creates, compiles and catalogs projects, know-how articles, standards, blue prints and applications for the purpose to make Mini and All-In-One Computer modular, ugradable and easy to repair. For the benefit of the individual DIY person as well as the industry as a whole. We have published what we have under CC and MIT License and we are working on closing all holes. Since this is Open Source, you are invited to help. Or simply use what is already available. Pay attention to the progress bars and ribbons.
