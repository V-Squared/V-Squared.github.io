Modular PC Open Source

Upgradable Mini and All-In-One PC

V-Squared is an Open Source Standard Initiative and Community on GitHub.com/V-Squared. We develop and publish standards, blueprints, know how articles, white papers and apps to make Mini and All-In-One Computer modular, ugradable and easy to repair. For the benefit of the individual user as well as the DIY Community and PC Industry as a whole.

The Missing Standard

Desktop Computer were the best selling PC form factor, because the ATX standard made them to be lowest cost, easy to build and upgrade. But this de facto standard is 30 years old and was not updated to fit the needs of Mini and All-In-One Form Factor. The V² Standard lowers cost, increases performance and simplifies upgrading. In doing so V² is breathing new life into Mini and All-In-One PCs.
